Vegetable Dalia (Cracked Wheat Porridge):
Vegetable Dalia (Cracked Wheat Porridge):

- Ingredients:

- 1 cup cracked wheat (dalia)
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans), finely chopped
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Dry roast cracked wheat in a pan until it turns golden brown and aromatic. Keep aside.
2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
5. Add mixed vegetables, turmeric powder, and salt. Sauté for a few minutes.
6. Add roasted cracked wheat and mix well.
7. Add 3 cups of water and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
8. Let the pressure release naturally. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Description:

Nutritious cracked wheat cooked with mixed vegetables and spices, a wholesome dinner option.

Nutrients:

About 280 calories, 8g protein, 40g carbohydrates, 10g fat, and 6g fiber per serving.

Time

Takes about 25-30 minutes to prepare.
