Vegetable Stir-fry with Tofu:

- Ingredients:

- 200g firm tofu, cubed
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell
peppers, broccoli, mushrooms, carrots) - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and ginger. Saut√© for 1 minute.
2. Add cubed tofu and stir-fry until lightly browned on all sides.
3. Add mixed vegetables and continue stir-frying for 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
4. Pour soy sauce over the stir-fry and toss to coat evenly.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Garnish with sesame seeds before serving.

Description:

A quick and colorful stir-fry of mixed vegetables and tofu, seasoned with soy sauce and garlic.

Nutrients:

Around 250 calories, 15g protein, 20g carbohydrates, 12g fat, and 6g fiber per serving.

Time

Takes about 20-25 minutes to prepare.
