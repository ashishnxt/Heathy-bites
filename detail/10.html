Paneer Bhurji (Scrambled Cottage Cheese):

- Ingredients:

- 200g paneer (cottage cheese), crumbled
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
- 2 green chilies, finely chopped
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
- Salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
- Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add finely chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
4. Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
6. Add crumbled paneer to the pan and mix gently with the masala.
7. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the paneer is heated through and well coated with the masala.
8. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
9. Serve hot with roti, paratha, or bread.

Description:

Spiced scrambled cottage cheese cooked with onions, tomatoes, and capsicum, perfect for a protein-packed lunch.

Nutrients:

Around 280 calories, 15g protein, 10g carbohydrates, 20g fat, and 4g fiber per serving.

Time

Takes about 20-25 minutes to prepare.
