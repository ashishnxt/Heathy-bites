Vegetable Idli:
Vegetable Idli:

- Ingredients:

- 1 cup idli batter
- 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans), finely chopped
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
- 1/2 teaspoon urad dal (black gram dal)
- A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
- Salt to taste
- Oil for greasing idli molds

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a small pan and add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add urad dal and asafoetida. Sauté until dal turns golden brown.
2. Add chopped vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool.
3. Grease idli molds with oil. Spoon a tablespoon of vegetable mixture into each mold.
4. Pour idli batter over the vegetable mixture, filling each mold.
5. Steam idlis in a steamer for 10-12 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
6. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.

Description:

Soft, steamed rice cakes packed with grated vegetables, perfect for a healthy start.

Nutrients:

Approximately 150 calories, 5g protein, 25g carbohydrates, 3g fat, and 4g fiber per serving.

Time

Takes about 30 minutes including fermentation time.
