Mixed Vegetable Soup:
Mixed Vegetable Soup:

- Ingredients:

- 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, corn)
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 teaspoon dried mixed herbs (oregano, thyme, rosemary)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a large pot. Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
3. Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
4. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes until vegetables are tender.
6. Season with dried herbs, salt, and pepper.
7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Description:

A hearty and nutritious soup made with assorted vegetables, flavored with herbs and spices.

Nutrients:

Approximately 150 calories, 5g protein, 20g carbohydrates, 5g fat, and 6g fiber per serving.

Time

Takes about 20-25 minutes to prepare.
