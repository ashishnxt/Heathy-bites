Moong Dal Chilla (Pancake):
Moong Dal Chilla (Pancake):

- Ingredients:

- 1 cup split yellow moong dal (split yellow mung beans) - 1 small onion, finely chopped - 1 green chili, finely chopped - 1/2 inch ginger, grated - 2 tablespoons chopped fresh coriander leaves - Salt to taste - 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds - Oil for cooking

Instructions:

1. Wash moong dal thoroughly and soak it in water for 4-5 hours or overnight. 2. Drain the soaked dal and transfer it to a blender. 3. Add chopped onion, green chili, ginger, coriander leaves, salt, and cumin seeds to the blender. 4. Blend the mixture into a smooth batter, adding a little water if necessary to achieve a thick pouring consistency. 5. Heat a non-stick skillet or tawa over medium heat. 6. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the skillet and spread it into a thin circle. 7. Drizzle a little oil around the edges of the chilla and cook until the bottom side turns golden brown. 8. Flip the chilla and cook the other side until golden brown and crispy. 9. Repeat the process with the remaining batter. 10. Serve hot with green chutney or yogurt.

Description:

Protein-rich pancakes made from ground moong dal (split green gram) batter, seasoned with spices.

Nutrients:

Around 180 calories, 8g protein, 25g carbohydrates, 5g fat, and 4g fiber per serving.

Time

Takes about 20-25 minutes to prepare.
