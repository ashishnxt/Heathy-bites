Vegetable Pulao:
Vegetable Pulao:

- Ingredients:

- 1 cup basmati rice
- 1 small onion, thinly sliced
- 1 small carrot, diced
- 1/2 cup green peas (fresh or frozen) - 1/2 cup diced bell peppers (assorted colors)
- 1/4 cup cauliflower florets
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 2 cloves
- 2 green cardamom pods
- 1-inch cinnamon stick
- 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
- Salt to taste
- 2 cups water
- Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
- Fried cashews and raisins for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1. Wash basmati rice under running water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 20-30 minutes. Drain and keep aside.
2. Heat ghee or oil in a deep pan or pressure cooker over medium heat.
3. Add cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom pods, and cinnamon stick. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
4. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
5. Add diced carrots, green peas, bell peppers, and cauliflower florets. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
6. Add soaked and drained basmati rice to the pan. Stir gently to coat the rice with the vegetables and spices.
7. Pour 2 cups of water into the pan. Season with salt to taste.
8. Cover the pan with a lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
9. Once done, fluff the rice gently with a fork.
10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and fried cashews and raisins (if using).
11. Serve hot with raita or any curry of your choice.

Description:

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices, a quick and satisfying lunch option

Nutrients:

About 320 calories, 6g protein, 50g carbohydrates, 8g fat, and 4g fiber per serving
Time

Takes about 30-35 minutes to prepare.
